# Cloud Spend Review Brief

A platform team prepared a quarterly cost review. Monthly cloud spend by service:

- Compute clusters: 42k
- Object storage: 18k
- Network egress: 9k
- Observability stack: 6k
- Build runners: 4k

The finance partner asked where cost-cutting conversations should start. The services are independent line items billed in parallel; none of them feeds into another, and the amounts are not stages of one shrinking population.
